[SCENE 1 — 0:00–0:12] The Problem
───────────────────────────────────
Visual: Indian SMB owner stressed at a desk, pile of papers, a contract
        with red marks crossed out.

NARRATOR (V.O.):
Every year, thousands of Indian businesses sign contracts they don't
fully understand.
Contracts that cost them money. Contracts that cost them rights.
Because good legal help is expensive — and most SMBs can't afford it.

────────────────────────────────
Visual: Telugu script  వాక్య  appears on screen, glowing gold.

వాక్య. Vaakya.
Telugu for "legal statement."
We built Vaakya to give every Indian business owner a voice
in every contract.

Eight specialized AI agents. Working like a full legal team — in minutes.

ARAMBHA      reads your request and understands exactly what you need.
RACHANA      drafts a complete, clause-perfect contract under Indian law.

Any document. Any deal. Under Indian Contract Act, 1872.
Professional. Enforceable. Yours — in under five minutes.

PRODUCTION NOTES
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Tone        : Confident, warm, Indian-rooted. Not corporate-cold.
Music       : South Indian classical motif opening → builds to modern

Language    : English narration. Telugu script and words on screen.
Palette     : #FEF9EF (cream), #1EA851 (green accent), #0F2D1F (dark text).
Agent cards : Use Cloudinary avatar images from the Vaakya dashboard.
Logo        : Vaakya wordmark + subtle leaf/contract icon.
Length      : 90 seconds. Keep tight — no filler.
